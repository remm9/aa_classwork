Descriptions

Shakshukah is a dish of eggs poached in a sauce of tomatoes, chili peppers, and onions, often spiced with cumin.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 medium brown or white onion, peeled and diced
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (or more to taste-- spicy!)
  • Pinch of sugar (optional, to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 5-6 eggs
  • 1/2 tbsp fresh chopped parsley (optional, for garnish)

Directions

  1. 1) chop up all ingredients
  2. heat up olive oil in a pan
  3. Add tomatoes and tomato paste to pan, stir till blended.
  4. Add spices and sugar, stir well, and allow mixture to simmer over medium heat for 5-7 minutes
